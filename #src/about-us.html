Our story

We are a team of people with backgrounds stemming from various countries around Europe. While each country has its individual nuances, we identified two problems that every country shares:

  • There are a lot of small factories around Europe that have difficulties in finding new clients.
  • It is extremely difficult to find a factory to produce your items, especially if you want to produce within Europe.

Our Solution

Sometimes complex problems are fixed with simple solutions. We wanted to create a solution that makes people think "That is so simple, why didn't I think of that?"

Our solution brings manufacturers of items and raw materials to buyers. No longer is there a need to invest heavily in search engine marketing and optimizing pages for SEO, both of which require a specialized set of skills and an incredible amount of money.

Our service is free to sign up and free to list your factory to. We aim to simplify the way business is done. Things shouldn't be difficult or hard to understand, they should be easy.
