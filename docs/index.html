Who are we, and what is this service?

Our goal is to save your time, energy, and most importantly, money. That's why, part of our "About Us" -text is right in the middle of the site!

Large factories in countries with cheap labor dominate the current production landscape. But what if you want to produce your products in small factories that are close to you? Before our service, it was very difficult to find these factories. Not anymore!

So, here is our promise; we will help you to find your new producers or your new buyers. Whether you own a factory, or you are looking for a factory, this is the place for you. The basic functions on this site are completely free!
